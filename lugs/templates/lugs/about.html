About Linux LUGs

LinuxLugs.com is a simple free and open-source directory for Linux User Groups and communities all around the world.

Using the site, you can find local Linux LUGs or communities and join the one you're interested in to stay up to date with its news and announcements.

You can also create/add your own LUG and invite members to join.
